Local Cat Elected Mayor for a Day

Residents Celebrate as Whiskers Takes Charge of Town Hall and Inspires Record-Breaking Naptime

By Tabby McWhiskers, Senior Correspondent

Residents of Maplewood were surprised and delighted on Monday when a local orange tabby named Whiskers was elected honorary mayor for the day. The friendly cat won by a landslide after receiving hundreds of votes from community members who praised his calm attitude and impressive napping skills.

During his term, Mayor Whiskers spent the morning greeting visitors and relaxing in a sunny window at Town Hall. Citizens reported a noticeable increase in happiness throughout the day, and town officials are already discussing whether another feline candidate should be considered in next year's election.
